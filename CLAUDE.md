## Getting Started

1. **Clone this template** — Create your own repo from this template
2. **Run setup** — `.\setup.ps1` to personalise your config
3. **Configure MCP** — Connect your Google Calendar and Gmail via Rube MCP
4. **Populate your KB** — Add team profiles, client info, and projects as you work
5. **Run `/startup`** — This will check your inbox, calendar, and surface actions
